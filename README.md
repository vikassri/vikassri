### About

I'm a **Solutions Architect** with deep experience in **enterprise data platforms**, **machine learning**, and **automation**. I enjoy turning complex distributed systems into **clear designs**, **secure patterns**, and **repeatable engineering**—from ingestion and processing to model delivery and observability.

- **Currently:** Building and advising on **Big Data & CDP-style** architectures; strong interest in **responsible ML** and **practical GenAI** integrations
- **Background:** ~18 years across data engineering, architecture, and hands-on delivery
- **Location:** Singapore
- **Ask me about:** **Cloudera / CDP**, **Hive & Spark tuning**, **data platform security**, Python/PySpark workflows, and cloud-native patterns
